Forever Yours
My Dearest Mami,
I've tried to write this letter a hundred times in my head, but every time I sit down to put it on paper, words feel too small to carry what I truly feel for you. You came into my life like a whisper I never saw coming, and suddenly the whole world felt louder, brighter, and more beautiful. You didn't just walk into my heart — you walked into my soul and made yourself at home. I love the way you laugh, the way your eyes light up when you talk about the things you love. I love how your voice sounds in the morning, soft and sleepy, like the universe is still humming a lullaby just for you. I love your strength — the way you carry the world on your shoulders and still find time to smile. I love your kindness — the way you care so deeply about everyone around you. I love your heart — because out of all the hearts in this world, it chose to love mine back. Mami, you make me want to be a better man. You make me believe in forever. You made me realize that love isn't just a word — it's the way I feel when I wake up knowing you exist in the same world as me. You are my favorite thought, my happiest memory, and my most beautiful dream. There are galaxies inside your eyes, and I want to spend the rest of my life exploring them. I want to be your peace, your home, your strength, and your biggest cheerleader. I want to laugh with you until our stomachs hurt, hold your hand through every storm, and dance with you in the middle of the kitchen when life feels simple and sweet. Mami, loving you has been the greatest adventure of my life. And I promise you this — I will never stop choosing you. Not today, not tomorrow, not ever. You are my always. Yours completely and forever,
~ Your Love ❤️
